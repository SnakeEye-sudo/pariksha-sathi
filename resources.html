Study Tips from Successful Candidates

Consistency is Key: Study for at least 4-6 hours daily. Consistency matters more than cramming for long hours occasionally.

Revision is Crucial: Allocate 40% of your time to revision. Create short notes for quick revision during the final months.

Practice Answer Writing: Practice writing answers regularly to improve writing speed and quality. Attempt previous year papers in exam conditions.

Stay Updated: Follow current affairs daily. Connect them with static subjects to enhance answer quality.

Maintain Health: Regular exercise and adequate sleep are essential. A healthy mind and body improve concentration and retention.

Join Study Groups: Discussing concepts with peers helps clarify doubts and provides new perspectives.
