🎯 What is ParikshaSathi?

ParikshaSathi is a revolutionary, completely FREE online study planner designed specifically for Indian competitive exam aspirants. Whether you're preparing for BPSC, UPSC, JEE, NEET, SSC, Banking exams, or any of 18+ competitive exams, ParikshaSathi provides you with a personalized, day-by-day study schedule that adapts to your needs.

We believe that quality education should be accessible to every aspirant, regardless of their financial background. That's why ParikshaSathi is completely free, with no hidden charges, no premium features, and no ads disrupting your learning.

⭐ Key Features

  • 📅 Personalized Study Plans: Get a customized day-by-day study schedule based on your exam date and available study hours.
  • 🔄 Smart Rotation System: Automatically rotates between subjects to maintain focus and prevent burnout.
  • 📊 Progress Tracking: Track your daily progress with beautiful visual representations.
  • 📥 PDF Download: Download your complete study plan as a PDF for offline access.
  • 🌙 Dark Mode: Comfortable studying with our dark-themed interface.
  • 📱 Mobile Friendly: Perfect experience on all devices - desktop, tablet, or mobile.
